Simple Shooting Game

An 18-Part Journey into Game Engineering & Collaborative AI Programming

The Vision

Can you build a complex, modular game without writing a single line of code? Yes. This project was entirely architected through incremental AI prompting. My goal was to show non-programmers and veterans alike that with the right guidance, AI can turn an idea into a functional, polished reality.

The Project

This series follows the evolution of a top-down shooting game built from scratch. It is designed for those who want to see the logic behind the code—moving from basic 2D movement to advanced autonomous AI behaviors, modular architecture, and persistent game states.

How It Was Built: AI Collaboration

0% Coding, 100% Architecture

Every function, collision check, and AI behavior was generated by AI tools. I acted as the Lead Architect—defining the roadmap, identifying the next feature, and overseeing the system growth.

Transparent Iteration

In Parts 1-11, I show the exact prompts used. You'll see the "back-and-forth" required to refine logic, showing that AI isn't a magic wand, but a powerful partner that requires clear direction.

The Build Workflow

I demonstrate the professional way to integrate AI code using Meld (File Compare). This allows us to see exactly what the AI changed versus the existing codebase, ensuring we stay in control of the project's evolution.

Intelligence
